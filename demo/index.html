How to read this. Every claim is tagged by provenance — measurement, prediction, literature (a real offline TF-IDF search over a curated knowledge base). A required measurement met only by a prediction is flagged, not accepted; a missing required measurement forces measure again, never “accept”. Research prototype — an evaluation & reproducibility layer, not a validated decision system.
